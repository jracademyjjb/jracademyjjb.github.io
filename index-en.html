About JR Academy JJB

JR ACADEMY JJB is more than a Jiu-Jitsu gym. It's a space of learning, respect, discipline, and personal growth. We welcome all ages with classes adapted to each student’s level, focusing on technique, safety, and well-being.

Teaching Methodology

Our methodology combines Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu tradition with modern teaching techniques. We offer technical training, conditioning, self-defense, controlled sparring, and support personal and mental development.

Benefits of Jiu-Jitsu

  • Improved physical fitness and endurance
  • Increased self-confidence
  • Discipline and focus
  • Emotional control and respect
  • Team spirit and social interaction

Who is Jiu-Jitsu for?

We offer classes for children from age 4, teens, adults, women, and beginners. We also provide private lessons for more personalized progress.
